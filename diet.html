Fueling Your Brain

For a student, food is fuel. Eating the right nutrients can improve memory, focus, and help you stay awake during long study sessions.

Recommended Daily Intake

Nutrient Source Student Benefit
Proteins Eggs, Beans, Nuts Brain cell repair
Omega-3 Walnuts, Fish, Seeds Improved Memory
Complex Carbs Oats, Whole Bread Steady Energy

Quick Healthy Tips

  • Avoid excessive sugar to prevent energy crashes.
  • Stay hydrated—dehydration causes fatigue.
  • Eat smaller, frequent meals to keep metabolism active.
