Stay Active, Stay Focused

Physical activity is not just for athletes. For students, it acts as a stress buster and helps improve blood flow to the brain.

3 Simple Habits:

  1. Morning Yoga: Spend 10 minutes stretching to wake up your body.
  2. The 50/10 Rule: For every 50 minutes of study, walk for 10 minutes.
  3. Evening Sports: Play a game like badminton or football to refresh your mind.
